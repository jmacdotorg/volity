Rock, Paper, Scissors

HomePage | RecentChanges | Preferences | Upload
A good balance of simplicity, familitarity, and brevity, Rock, Paper, Scissors (RPS for short) is the epitome of test rulesets on the Volity platform, and as such appears in many examples throughout Volity's documentation (including this Wiki).

Its ruleset URI is http://volity.org/games/rps , which not coincidentally also points to a website definiting the ruleset's protocol.

We also run a permanent RPS test server at rps@volity.net/volity .
