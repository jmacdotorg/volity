Jabber Entity

By Jabber entity, we mean any distinct connection to a Jabber network, identifiable by its unique JabberID. It could be a person, or a program.

This concept is very important to Volity, whose major system components -- including parlors, referees, tables and players -- all take the form of Jabber entities. Without exception, all communication between them happens according to the standard Jabber/XMPP protocol, with JabberIDs used for addressing.
